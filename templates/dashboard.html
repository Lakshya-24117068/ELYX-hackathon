Welcome to the ELYX Family

Your personalized health journey starts here. Explore key wellness indicators and tips to help you stay on track.

Blood Pressure

Aim for a reading below 120/80 mmHg. This helps protect against heart disease and stroke.

Daily Sleep

Strive for 7-9 hours of quality sleep per night to support recovery, cognitive function, and mood.

Resting Heart Rate

A healthy range for adults is typically between 60 and 100 bpm. Lower rates often indicate better fitness.

Hydration

Drink around 8 glasses (2 liters) of water daily. Adjust based on your activity level and the climate.

Mindful Minutes

Incorporate 10-15 minutes of mindfulness or meditation daily to reduce stress and improve focus.

Daily Activity

Aim for at least 8,000 steps or 30 minutes of moderate exercise most days of the week.

Balanced Nutrition

Fill half your plate with fruits & vegetables, a quarter with lean protein, and a quarter with whole grains.

Strength Training

Incorporate 2-3 sessions per week to build muscle mass, strengthen bones, and boost your metabolism.
